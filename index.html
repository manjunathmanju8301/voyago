02 / How it works

Plan, book, go. In three steps.

No onboarding tour. No "create your first trip" wizard. You search, you pick, you pay — like a normal website, except it actually does all five categories.

STEP / 01

Search anything, anywhere.

Type a city, a date, or just "tonight". We pull inventory from buses, trains, flights, hotels and movies in one shot, ranked by what's actually best for you.

STEP / 02

Pick how you want to travel.

Compare side by side: prices, durations, layovers, legroom. Stack legs from different operators into a single itinerary with one QR code at the end.

STEP / 03

Pay once. Done.

One checkout, one receipt, one place to change or cancel everything. We handle the operator sync so you don't end up with five confirmation emails.

09 / Questions

The things people actually ask.

No glossing over. The real questions, the real answers.

Do you charge booking fees?
No markup on the operator's listed price. The first three trips are free; after that we charge a flat ₹49 per booking, regardless of how many legs are bundled. We tell you the total before you pay — never after.
What if my bus or train is cancelled?
We monitor every active booking in real time. If the operator cancels or delays a leg past 45 minutes, we auto-reroute you onto the next available option and notify you in-app. If nothing works within two hours, we issue a refund without you having to ask.
How does the AI helpdesk actually book things?
It's built on Mastra and has direct integration with every operator's booking API. It can search, hold (a temporary 10-minute reservation), pay (via your stored methods), and refund. It never does anything without showing you what it's about to do and getting your confirmation.
Can I book for someone else?
Yes. Add up to six traveler profiles (each with their own ID, seat preference and dietary notes). On the checkout screen, pick who each ticket is for. They get a separate QR code and SMS, but you keep the master view and can cancel on their behalf.
What if the operator doesn't accept online cancellation?
We process the refund on the operator's behalf. About 6% of bookings still require a phone call to the operator (mostly regional bus companies); we make that call for you, in the local language, and update you when the refund is confirmed.
Is Pro worth it for someone who travels twice a year?
Probably not. At ₹299/month you'd save more by paying ₹49 per booking. The math flips around the 6th booking of the year — most of our Pro subscribers are people who travel monthly for work or family.
